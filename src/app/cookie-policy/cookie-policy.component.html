StreamCinema Cookie Policy

Cookies are small pieces of text stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you are on the Internet, including on StreamCinema's website.

We use cookies to understand how you use our site, improve your experience, and personalize content. In general, the cookies on our site are used for the following purposes:

Authentication: If you're signed in to StreamCinema, cookies help us show you the right information and personalize your experience.
Security: We use cookies to enable and support security features, and to help us detect malicious activity.
Preferences, features, and services: Cookies can help you fill out forms on StreamCinema. They also provide you with features, insights, and customized content.
Marketing: We may use cookies to help us deliver marketing campaigns and track their performance.

Most web browsers are set to accept cookies by default. However, you can choose to set your browser to remove or reject cookies. Please note that if you choose to remove or reject cookies, this could affect the availability and functionality of StreamCinema.
